Great question! Eevee is Blender's real-time render engine. It's very fast and great for previews, stylized looks, and game assets. Cycles is a path-tracing render engine that's more physically accurate, producing more realistic lighting, shadows, and reflections, but it generally takes longer to render. Many artists use Eevee for look development and viewport previews, then switch to Cycles for final high-quality renders. We'll have articles diving deeper into both!
